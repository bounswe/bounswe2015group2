Project Description

[http://www.kosherhomeschool.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/aa-kids-healthy-eat.jpg]

In today's world, we are provided with a wide range of foods not only by food provider companies, but also by lots of restaurants, cafes etc. But, we can't decide what to eat or what to cook because we have little information about nutrients of a food. In order to come up with a solution to this problem, we are going to develop a crowd sourcing application linking the food providers and consumers of food based on healthy data.

This project is an awareness tool and nutritional assistant for its users while linking them according to their food social network.
